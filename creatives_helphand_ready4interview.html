ARE YOU READY FOR THE
INTERVIEW?

Ok there is a 8 step sequence to get you ready for the interview.

  • Research everything about the company.

  • Learn and answer basic interview questions

  • i have done a practice interview and have gotten feedback from a friend or family member

  • i know which of my skills most relate to the job, and how to communicate this to the interviewer.

  • Have i tried on my interview outfit? And it is free of holes? Ironed and makes me feel comfortable and confident?

  • I have prepared at least 5 interview questions I am going to ask the employer/Interviewer

  • I have a pen and paper, I know how to turn my phone off and know the directions to the interview so I am there on time

  • I am going to email and thank my interviewer for their time.
